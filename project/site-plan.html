Site plan - Pelis (Movie Gallery)

Site Purpose:

  • Movie Gallery from The Movie Database.
  • Ability to create a favorite movies List.
  • Every movie will have details like, release date, director, duration, cast, etc.
  • Filters of release date, category, etc.

Scenario:

  • What are the top-ranked movies to watch this weekend?
  • Can I save a list of movies I want to watch later?
  • What are the more rated action movies?
